Privacy Policy for Web Highlighter & Notes

Last Updated: March 2024

Introduction

Web Highlighter & Notes is a Chrome extension that allows users to highlight text and take notes on web pages. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data and what information we collect.

Information We Collect

Our extension collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Text highlights you create on web pages
  • Notes you add to your highlights
  • The URLs of pages where you create highlights
  • Page titles where highlights are created

How We Use Your Information

All data collected by the extension is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not:

  • Send any data to external servers
  • Share your highlights or notes with third parties
  • Track your browsing history
  • Collect any personal information

Data Storage

Your highlights and notes are stored in your browser's local storage. This means:

  • Data is only accessible on your device
  • Data persists until you clear your browser data or uninstall the extension
  • Data is not synchronized across devices unless you use Chrome's sync feature

Permissions We Use

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • storage: To save your highlights and notes locally
  • activeTab: To access the current tab for highlighting
  • scripting: To inject highlighting functionality
  • downloads: To export highlights as PDF or Markdown files
  • tabs: To access tab information for organizing highlights

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can delete all your highlights and notes by clearing the extension's data
  • You can uninstall the extension at any time
  • You can export your highlights and notes as PDF or Markdown files

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the extension's GitHub repository.
